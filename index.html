Hey there! I'm Shivangi, a seasoned UI/UX and product designer based in the charming corners of New Jersey.For over a decade, I've been crafting interfaces that are not just visually pleasing but also as user-friendly as finding your favorite socks on a Monday morning.

When I'm not busy sketching wireframes or playing with color palettes, you'll find me indulging my passion for interior design. I'm all about rearranging furniture, collecting quirky decor, and turning spaces into cozy havens.

Oh, and I share my home with my husband and two furballs who keep life playful and exciting!
